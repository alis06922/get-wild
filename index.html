What is WildToken ($WILD)?

WildToken ($WILD) is a cryptocurrency designed for real-world use, allowing users to earn, spend, and trade it across various platforms. It will be integrated into an app where users can earn rewards for engaging with content, holding $WILD, and clipping media.

How can I buy $WILD?

You can buy $WILD in three simple steps:

Set up & fund your wallet – Use a Phantom wallet and add Solana (SOL). Swap SOL for $WILD – Connect to a DEX like Raydium or Jupiter and swap SOL for $WILD. Hold, spend, or trade – Your $WILD will be in your wallet, ready for use.

How do I earn $WILD?

You can earn WildToken in three ways:

Clipping content – Get rewarded for sharing and clipping videos. Engaging on social media – Earn $WILD for liking, sharing, and interacting with content. Holding $WILD – Simply holding $WILD in your wallet unlocks passive earnings.

Where can I use $WILD?

WildToken will be usable for purchasing exclusive merchandise, toys, and digital services, with more real-world integrations coming soon.
